China

DBS has been innovating for nearly 50 years, with many achievements and milestones for the bank. We've collected some of our finest moment here. Do scroll through them and enjoy!

2016

Inked four strategic partnerships at the MOUs Signing Ceremony of the “China-Singapore (Chongqing) Demonstration Initiative on Strategic Connectivity”.

2015

First foreign bank in China to successfully issue a Basel III Tier 2 capital bond.

2014

First foreign bank to sign a strategic cooperation agreement with Shanghai International Energy Exchange Limited (“INE”) to boost the internalization of China’s energy futures exchange market.

2012

Launched a social enterprise programme to support the development of social entrepreneurship in China and rolled out China’s first social enterprise banking package.

2010

DBS Bank Tower, our first building in China, was inaugurated in the heart of Shanghai Financial area.

2016

Officially launched a 15-year regional distribution agreement with Manulife Financial Asia Limited covering China, Singapore, Hong Kong, and Indonesia.

2015

First batch of banks selected to be a member bank of China’s first international payments system, designated to execute RMB clearing for all cross-border RMB transactions.

2014

Pioneered a RMB denominated loan from our Singapore headquarters under the new SIP cross-border renminibi transactions initiative, for one of our customers based in Suzhou Industrial Park (SIP).

2011

Launched inaugural DBS-Fudan Banking Programme jointly with Shanghai Fudan University to identify talent for the bank.

2007

First few foreign banks and the first Singapore bank to incorporate in China as DBS Bank (China) Limited.

Hong Kong

DBS has been innovating for nearly 50 years, with many achievements and milestones for the bank. We've collected some of our finest moment here. Do scroll through them and enjoy!

2016

Launched the DBS Omni Credit Card Companion Mobile App, leveraging digital innovation to bring about a safe, secure and joyful banking experience for DBS credit card customers.

2014

Awarded Gold for Outstanding Customer Service Programme by the Hong Kong Association for Customer Service Excellence (HKACE).

2011

Launched DBS Treasures Private Client in Hong Kong as an extension of DBS Private Bank offering, targeting clients with investible assets of HK$ 8 million and above.

2009

Launched our flagship community programme ‘DBS Social Enterprise Experience’, providing a learning platform for youths to hone their business skills and cultivate social entrepreneurship.

2005

Launched an ATM card that can access four major payment networks - China UnionPay (CUP), JETCO, PLUS and EPS, allowing our customers to make payments and withdrawals at ATMs and retailers in mainland China and around the world.

2015

Allocated HKD 2 million to launch DBS Social Enterprise Advancement Grant 2015 - Social Innovation Programme in Hong Kong, through our Singapore-based DBS Foundation.

2013

Launched uGOiGO, a group-buy ingenuity and innovation campaign to drive deposits from affluent customers via social media.

2010

Launched the first-in-town Octopus ATM Card, which combines the functions of an ATM card with the Octopus public transport farecard in one piece of plastic.

2009

First bank in Hong Kong to introduce a Dual Password Authentication by sending out One-Time Password via SMS.

2003

Completed the legal merger of Dao Heng Bank Ltd, DBS Kwong On Bank Ltd and Overseas Trust Bank, and adopted the new legal name "DBS Bank (Hong Kong) Limited" and the new Chinese name 星展銀行(香港)有限公司.

India

DBS has been innovating for nearly 50 years, with many achievements and milestones for the bank. We've collected some of our finest moment here. Do scroll through them and enjoy!

2017

Defied banking industry norms by recruiting 100 technology skilled professionals, who are professionals in emerging and disruptive technologies across cloud, machine learning and Big Data, through a hackathon.

2016

Launched DBS Asia Hub 2, DBS’ newly established technology development centre in Hyderabad, Telangana, and our bank’s largest technology hub outside Singapore.

2015

Converted our branch licence to Wholly Owned Subsidiary (WOS) in India.

2009

Entered into a partnership with the State Bank of India (SBI), India's largest bank, to offer an online Remittance service from Singapore to India.

2005

Launched our Wealth Management Business.

2017

Launched DBS Ideathon, a first-of-its kind initiative across the banking industry where premiere campuses across the country converged together on a digital platform to pitch ideas and solutions that matter to the financial world.

2016

Unveiled digibank, India’s first mobile-only bank. A revolutionary offering, digibank brings together an entire suite of ground-breaking technology – from biometrics to artificial intelligence (AI) – to enable customers to enjoy a whole new way of banking.

2011

Launched DBS iBanking, our first ever Online Banking Platform for Individual customers.

2008

Set up eight new branches in India by the Reserve Bank of India across Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, Moradabad, Nasik, Pune, Salem and Surat, bringing DBS Bank's India presence to a total of 10 branches.

1995

Banking business commenced with our office in Maker Chambers IV at Nariman Point in Mumbai

Indonesia

DBS has been innovating for nearly 50 years, with many achievements and milestones for the bank. We've collected some of our finest moment here. Do scroll through them and enjoy!

2016

Officially launched a 15-year regional distribution agreement with Manulife Financial Asia Limited covering China, Singapore, Hong Kong, and Indonesia.

2016

Held the first DBS Bring Indonesia to Global (DBS BIG), a business model competition for Small and Medium Enterprises (SME) and Social Enterprises (SE) in creative sector in Indonesia, with the Indonesia Creative Economy Agency (BEKRAF).

2015

Held the DBS Young Economist Stand Up Contest, a bi-annual program targeting university student across Indonesia. Finalists were given a chance to showcase their talent at DBS Indonesia's annual Asian Insights Conference.

2011

Successfully hooked up to the ATM Bersama network, thereby allowing our customers to reach over 30,000 ATMs.

2016

Acquired the wealth management and retail banking business of ANZ in five markets, Singapore, Hong Kong, China, Taiwan and Indonesia, including 1.3 million affluent/ private and retail customers.

2016

Launched DBS BusinessClass, a platform that helps business grow and network, to receive the latest market trends and gain exclusive access to a knowledgeable community of Asia's business masterminds.

2014

Sealed a partnership with 3 insurance companies, PT Allianz Utama Indonesia, PT Asuransi Astra Buana and PT Asuransi Adira Dinamika to complete our bancassurance product offerings.

2013

Realocated our Head Office to DBS Bank Tower located at Ciputra World.

2012

Introduced prestigous new benefits of Concierge Servise, SOS international and a lifestyle magazine dedicated to DBS Treasures customers.

1997

DBS Indonesia was officially incorporated and launched operations in Surabaya, expanding our presence in the East of Java Island, offering corporate banking products.

Singapore

DBS has been innovating for nearly 50 years, with many achievements and milestones for the bank. We've collected some of our finest moment here. Do scroll through them and enjoy!

2016

Introduced DBS digibank, India’s first paperless, signatureless, branches, mobile-only bank

2014

First Asian bank to pilot the use of artificial intelligence, launched Singapore’s first mobile wallet

2002

Launched the first REIT in Asia ex-Japan

1998

Post Office Savings Bank (POSB) is welcomed to the DBS family on 16 November 1998

1995

Launched DBS’ official website, the first bank in Singapore to do so

1986

Financed the development of Raffles City, paving the way for Singapore to be a leading convention and exhibition destination.

1985

DBS also pioneered the NETS system and boasts the largest network of ATMs amongst commercial banks in Singapore

1977

Launched Pandan Valley condominium, Singapore’s first condominium

1974

Launched Singapore’s first ATM

2015

First in Singapore to incorporate hackathons into our talent development programme

2010

Introduced DBS’ suite of mobile banking apps, changing the way consumers bank-on-the go

2001

Introduced micro-loans for local start-ups and SMEs

1997

Introduced Singapore’s first internet banking platform revolutionising the local banking scene

1993

Introduced electronic share application through ATMs

1985

Propelled Singapore Airlines onto the global market by being the lead manager for their IPO, the first international offering for a locally incorporated company

1980

Introduced DBS Autosave, the first interest-bearing current account in Singapore

1975

Financed the development of Plaza Singapura, the country’s first multi-storey shopping mall and pioneered the foreign anchor tenant concept with Yaohan

1968

The Development Bank of Singapore is incorporated as a public listed company on 16 July 1968

Taiwan

DBS has been innovating for nearly 50 years, with many achievements and milestones for the bank. We've collected some of our finest moment here. Do scroll through them and enjoy!

2016

Launched digital initiatives such as Home Advisor and DBS BusinessClass to retail and coporate clients, towards a joyful banking experience.

2012

DBS Bank's subsidiary in Taiwan, DBS Bank (Taiwan), commenced operations at the beginning of the year.

2011

Inaugurated our new Taiwan headquarters at Shin Kong Xinyi Financial Center at the heart of Taipei’s financial district.

2008

Acquired BOWA Bank’s “good assets”, the first Singapore bank to make a major investment in the country.

2016

Crowned as the Best Talent Development Award, recognizing our efforts in the talent incubaton.

2013

Started to target social enterprises as our main focus for corporate social responsibility to bring positive synergy to the society and create a long-term continuous impact.

2012

Set up two new DBS SME Banking Centers, Banxin and Jixian SME centers located in New Taipei City

2010

Re-located our technology operations and support staff to Taipei Hub in the Neihu Science Park, featuring state-of-the-art technology, training classrooms and a social hub for recreational use.

1983

First established a presence in Taiwan in 1983, providing banking products and services for corporate clients.
