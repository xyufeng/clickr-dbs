China

DBS has been innovating for nearly 50 years, with many achievements and milestones for the bank. We've collected some of our finest moment here. Do scroll through them and enjoy!

2016

Inked four strategic partnerships at the MOUs Signing Ceremony of the “China-Singapore (Chongqing) Demonstration Initiative on Strategic Connectivity”.

2015

First foreign bank in China to successfully issue a Basel III Tier 2 capital bond.

2014

First foreign bank to sign a strategic cooperation agreement with Shanghai International Energy Exchange Limited (“INE”) to boost the internalization of China’s energy futures exchange market.

2012

Launched a social enterprise programme to support the development of social entrepreneurship in China and rolled out China’s first social enterprise banking package.

2010

DBS Bank Tower, our first building in China, was inaugurated in the heart of Shanghai Financial area.

2016

Officially launched a 15-year regional distribution agreement with Manulife Financial Asia Limited covering China, Singapore, Hong Kong, and Indonesia.

2015

First batch of banks selected to be a member bank of China’s first international payments system, designated to execute RMB clearing for all cross-border RMB transactions.

2014

Pioneered a RMB denominated loan from our Singapore headquarters under the new SIP cross-border renminibi transactions initiative, for one of our customers based in Suzhou Industrial Park (SIP).

2011

Launched inaugural DBS-Fudan Banking Programme jointly with Shanghai Fudan University to identify talent for the bank.

2007

First few foreign banks and the first Singapore bank to incorporate in China as DBS Bank (China) Limited.
