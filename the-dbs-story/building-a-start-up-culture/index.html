CREATING A 22,000 PERSON START-UP
Building a
start up culture

Our ability to attract, retain and develop the best talent is the key to our continued success.

We are committed to creating a great workplace for employees to live fulfilled the way they define it – to be the best, to be the change and to be the difference. As we lead our journey to reimagine banking, our people can impact lives and make a difference.

our pride! values

At DBS, the right person for the right job means someone who possesses both technical expertise and alignment with our PRIDE! values. As we embark on our digital journey and move to meet the growing needs of our customers, we have started to bring in talent who are agile in the way they think and can apply the practical skills of Human Centred Design (HCD) and Agile project management as they operate in the new world.

Driving culture transformation is a continuous journey. When we reviewed our values in 2014, we focused on creating awareness about the refreshed PRIDE! values and embedding this in everything we do. Based on the employee engagement survey by Aon Hewitt in 2016, employee ratings for PRIDE! and Customer Focus have improved, outperforming the Aon Best Employer norms.

Driving culture transformation is a continuous journey. When we reviewed our values in 2014, we focused on creating awareness about the refreshed PRIDE! values and embedding this in everything we do. Based on the employee engagement survey by Aon Hewitt in 2016, employee ratings for PRIDE! and Customer Focus have improved, outperforming the Aon Best Employer norms.

At the heart of all we do, we want our people to see beyond their day-to-day and be guided by the corporate values – to take pride in how they do their business and be recognised for their commitment, innovation and sense of empowerment.
A Strong
DBS Team

In Singapore, for instance, 85% of employees are aligned to the ‘Purpose-driven’ value, indicating that they find DBS’ mission meaningful – this is 15% stronger than the Aon APAC Best Employer norm. We are also 20% above the Aon APAC Best Employers norm for our strategic priority on ‘Embracing the Digital World’.

Embracing and embedding PRIDE! continues to be a key priority – it is the foundation to everything that we do. As we transform into a 22,000-person start-up, one of the key thrusts is to leverage PRIDE! – to encourage our people to be the change that will make DBS the Best Bank in the World.
